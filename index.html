What is in cosmalverse?

This is an extreme RPG expansion of Minecraft, meaning you can go out into the world, craft, mine, build houses, just like normal Minecraft, except with a twist...

A main campaign has been added, featuring over 40+ dungeons, 200+ custom mobs, a full custom world with amazing terrain, caves and builds, and over 50+ unique bosses in a long and exciting adventure.

Before joining, we recommend you install our resource pack. This adventure is available on java or bedrock, meaning you can even play this on your phone.
